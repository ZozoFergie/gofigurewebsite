Built to solve real skater problems

For skaters working toward Learn To Skate or USFS tests — and the coaches and parents supporting them.

📄

Test Requirements, Finally Clear

Stop hunting through PDFs or stumbling on outdated versions online. Go Figure puts accurate, up-to-date USFS and Learn To Skate test requirements right in your pocket.

🎥

A Video Library Built for Skaters

Search by element name and find real examples of exactly what you're working on. No more guessing — see it done right, then go do it yourself.

📈

See How Far You've Come

Build a personal archive of your elements over time. Watch yourself improve, share clips with your coach, and never wonder if you're making progress again.

❄

A Community That Gets It

Connect with other skaters at your level. Find inspiration, cheer each other on, and remember you're not doing this alone.

Made for skaters who love the sport

You don't have to be chasing the Olympics to deserve great tools. Go Figure is for the skater who shows up every week, works hard in practice, and wants a clear picture of where they're headed.

Whether you're a skater, a coach, or a parent watching from the boards — Go Figure gives everyone the visibility and support they need to keep going.

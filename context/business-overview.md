# Go Figure — Business Overview

## What We're Building
An iOS app called **Go Figure**, initially targeting figure skaters of all ages, their coaches, and parents.

## Target Customer
- Skates **1x/week or more**, recreational to competitive (not necessarily elite)
- Working toward a **Learn To Skate** or **USFS test**
- Likely a member of Learn To Skate USA or US Figure Skating

## Problems We're Solving

1. **Test requirements are hard to find**
   Skaters and coaches waste time hunting through PDFs or finding outdated info online. Go Figure centralizes accurate, up-to-date test requirements in one place.

2. **No good video reference library for elements**
   Skaters need to see many examples of individual elements to improve and learn new ones. No current platform is extensive or accurately searchable by element name.

3. **Skaters quit when they can't see progress or feel community**
   Without visible growth and connection to others who share the sport, recreational skaters disengage and drop out.

## Core Value Proposition
Improve retention for non-elite skaters by providing:
- **Clear path forward** — structured view of next steps and test progression
- **Proof of progress** — personal video archive of elements over time
- **Creative inspiration** — discover new ways to enjoy the sport
- **Community** — an audience of other skaters who gets it
